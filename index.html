About Archie's


Treating our customers like family is in our blood, we want everyone to feel welcome! For years our family has served the pacific northwest in the form of various family owned restaurants, all inspired from our home base of Jalisco, Mexico. In 2008, we sold our restaurant in Des Moines "Lago Azul Mexican Restaurant", but it did not take long to realize how much we missed our customers. "Lago Azul" was often called "Archie's Place" by our regular customers and friends, after our founder and manager Argelia "Archie" Armando Sahagun. Naturally when the time came to think of a name for a new restaurant - it was clear there was only one answer, Archie's Mexican Restaurant.


Here at Archie's, we want to accommodate your dietary needs, wants and restrictions - please don't hesitate to ask us to customize you meal! Archie's offers a variety of Authentic Mexican dishes as well as Vegetarian and Pescatarian options. Every meal is cooked from scratch, because we don't believe in taking shortcuts, you deserve the best.

While we are primarily a family restaurant, but we do offer a full bar that is reservable, with a patio and projection screen TV for our 21+ customers. Reservations can be made for family events in our Banquet room, which is able to be sectioned off for private parites.
